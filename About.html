About InterGalactic
InterGalactic is more than just another destination—it's a gateway to the cosmos, open for everyone. Founded by Istiak Erik, a passionate astrophile and physics enthusiast, our website is dedicated to presenting the wonders of the cosmos and the scientific world in a way that ignites curiosity and inspires scientific exploration.
Reason of creating this Website
I believe, the best way to set a certain topic into your brain for a long time is to try to teach that topic to someone who is unaware of that topic. Since I'm very much passionate about science and physics, I try to learn new things everyday. And this is the place where I'm going to post those topics. I really don't care if no one visits and reads my posts. Additionally, I'm not interested in making money from this, if i had, i would host this website and buy a domain for this. In fact, since the website is not hosted, I won't have any idea of how much traffic is there (Means how many people is visiting).
Our Mission
At InterGalactic, we believe that the mysteries of the scientific world and the cosmos should be accessible to all. Our mission is to make the complexities of space not only understandable but also captivating, encouraging everyone to delve deeper into the beauty and intricacies of the universe.

Contribute to InterGalactic

Why Contribute?

By sharing your expertise with InterGalactic, you'll have the opportunity to showcase your passion for the cosmos and inspire others to explore the wonders of space. As a contributor, you'll receive author rights in the post, acknowledging your valuable contribution to our community.

What We're Looking For

We welcome articles on a wide range of topics related to astronomy, physics, and space science. Whether you want to delve into the latest discoveries, share tips for stargazing, or explore the mysteries of the universe, we encourage you to submit your ideas!

How to Get Involved?

To contribute an article, simply reach out to us with your proposed topic or completed draft. Our editorial team will review your submission and work with you to prepare it for publication on InterGalactic. Together, let's inspire curiosity and exploration in the cosmos!

Join Us at InterGalactic

Join our community of astronomy enthusiasts and share your cosmic insights with the world! Together, we can ignite curiosity and deepen our understanding of the universe.
